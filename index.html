Tableflip

A conference on design, play and tabletop games.

San Francisco · October 4th—5th

At TableFlip, there's no exhibit hall, no tournaments and no huge crowds. Instead, four talented game designers will present in-depth case studies of unique tabletop games.

After exploring game mechanics and theme in detail, attendees experience each game in group play sessions — with tons of time for discussion the art and practice of game design.

Pandemic, Forbidden Desert
